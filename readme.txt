== Description ==
<p>SeeVolution is a Customer Experience web analytic tool that allows you to understand visitors’ behavior on your website using a smart and friendly overly-toolbar.<br.>This release includes the following features:</p>
<ul>
<li>	<b>Site and Page Analytics:</b> analytics tool tracking your overall clicks</li>
<li>	<b>Click Heatmap:</b> See the hot/cold spots, where on each page your visitors engage</li>
<li>	<b>Eye Tracking:</b> See visitor's’ mouse movements on the page</li>
<li>	Attention scroll heat map - find the fold for every page. See how far down each page your users scroll.</li>
<li>	<b>Advanced Filters:</b> filter results by device type, referrers or geo location.</li>
<li>	<b>Live Clicks:</b> Get instant feedback on design changes and campaigns. Each click will appear LIVE on the map. You can zoom in/out to get a better view at your audience’s geo-location. You can also filter your view by visitors’ interaction with specific goals.</li>
<li>	<b>Recorded Sessions:</b> provides an up close and personal view of your user's’ experience. No buffers.Efficiently tweak specific scenarios using filtered recorded sessions by country, number of pages viewed, and session duration. You can see the exact geo location of every recorded session.</li>
<li>	<b>Backstage views:</b> Zoom in on your website’s hot spots to see who are the visitors engaging your hot spot: where they come from, how did they get to your site, what platform and browsers they’re using, and more.<br>Select the Crosshair view to dive deep into a specific point, or the Perimeter cursor view to see detailed information for a specific area on the page.
</li>
<li>	<b>Asset analysis:</b> Creating a good web page is not about using a giant brush. It’s about each and every element of your page and the way it all comes together to provide an engaging, goal oriented customer experience. Our Asset analysis feature will show you how engaging each image, link and form is, and in real time. Perfection is in details...</li>
<li>	<b>Form analytic:</b> Improve customer subscription. Optimize forms to ensure visitors hit Submit. See how many users completed each field and how long it took</li>
<li>	<b>Goals:</b> Set your goals & see how your campaigns perform. Did you target the right audience? Did it meet your expectations? Is your site designed to support your goals? Get your answers by setting up simple yet effective goals and see your visitors’ funnel engagement.</li>
</ul>
== Installation ==
1. Open a SeeVolution account on https://console.seevolution.com/Plans.aspx
2. Login and add your domain name to your seevolution accoun. Go to the installation page for instructions: https://www.seevolution.com/installing-seevolution
3. There is no need to copy the code snippet to your WordPress site as this plugin takes care of it automatically!
4. Upload the SeeVolution plugin to your Wordpress site
5. Activate the plugin
6. Enjoy!
